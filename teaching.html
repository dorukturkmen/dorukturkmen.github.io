Interaction Design II

The aim of this course is to enable students to analyze complex and ambiguous design problems from a critical perspective and to develop creative, reflective, and alternative interaction scenarios using speculative design and narrative techniques.

View Syllabus
Fall 2025

Digital Game Design

This course explores the fundamentals of video game design with a focus on the integration of visual communication principles. Students will learn to design, conceptualize, and develop an engaging “small video game” with an emphasis on imparting meaning unto player experience. They will utilize established game design theories into their own design and development process and enrich their skill sets by doing so.
